Methodology

Data is sourced from Wellington City Council's annual remuneration disclosure, which councils are required to publish under the Local Government Act 2002. Under this requirement, councils must report the number of employees in each $20,000 salary band.

This list uses the mid-point of each reported salary band as the basis for comparison and ranking. Where an employee's name and role could be identified from publicly available sources — including the council's own website, annual reports, and meeting minutes — that information has been included.

Salary figures are for the year ended 30 June 2024. Not all positions in each band could be identified by name; unnamed positions are excluded from this list.
